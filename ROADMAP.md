## üí° Personal Branding Tips

1. **Be Authentic**: Let your personality shine through
2. **Tell Stories**: Don't just list‚Äîexplain the journey and impact
3. **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Demos, screenshots, and live projects
4. **Keep It Updated**: An outdated portfolio is worse than no portfolio
5. **Make It Personal**: Add unique touches that represent you
6. **Focus on Impact**: Highlight results and value delivered
7. **Easy to Contact**: Remove friction for opportunities
8. **Professional Yet Human**: Balance credentials with personality
9. **Mobile-First**: Many recruiters browse on phones
10. **Fast & Accessible**: Technical excellence reflects on you
